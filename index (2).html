      How to use this

      Everything you need to know, in one place.

      First time here

      1. Go to Campaigns → Upload Beauty Pass calendar to import all your campaigns at once from the BP file.
      2. Go to Campaigns → Upload tracker sheet to import your existing Creator Network Post Calendar — each FGF tab becomes a campaign with all its creators, statuses, and post links.
      3. Open any campaign, click Upload comp list to add Anna's comp list with tracking numbers for that campaign.
      4. Go to Creator roster to manage your master list of creators. The X button removes them from the roster permanently — it does NOT remove them from any campaigns.

      Weekly workflow

      1. Friday AM: Open the campaign, upload comp list from Anna, click "✉ Friday tracking emails" — Outlook opens once per creator, each pre-filled with their tracking number.
      2. Wednesday: Click "✉ Wednesday reminder" — only sends to creators who haven't submitted.
      3. After due date: Overview shows late creators. Use "What's missing" on the Overview to select late creators and generate the follow-up email.
      4. NuSkin / campaigns with no date: Open the campaign and type the FGF date — all other dates will fill in automatically.

      Sending emails

      1. Every email button opens Outlook with the subject, body, and recipient already filled in.
      2. The ✉ icon on each creator row opens a menu to pick which type of email to send to just that person.
      3. Bulk buttons send to a filtered group — Wednesday reminder skips people who already submitted, follow-ups skip people who aren't overdue, etc.
      4. You always review before sending. Nothing goes out automatically.

      Your data

      1. Everything saves automatically as you type. No account or login needed.
      2. Data lives in this browser on this computer. Don't clear browser data or you'll lose it.
      3. Export a backup occasionally using the buttons below — save the file somewhere safe.
      4. Right-click this tab → Pin tab so it stays visible all day.
